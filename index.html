Prioritizing the Regulatory Noncoding Genome for Disease Causal Variant in Retinal Diseases | Python, R

Baylor College of Medicine, Jan 2023 - May 2023

Presented poster at the 2023 Rice Undergraduate Research Symposium.

kNN Document Classification | Apache Spark, Python (PySpark, NumPy), AWS

Rice University, April 2023

Used RDDs to extract features from 20,000 text documents by computing a bag-of-words model. Computed TF-IDF features to weight word importance. Built and implemented kNN classifier to predict document categories on an AWS EMR cluster.

Bacterial Infection Transmission Map | Python

Rice University, March 2023 - April 2023

Designed algorithm and built program to compute a rooted, directed minimum spanning tree to model a bacterial infection outbreak and predict the spread of infection in a hospital. Inferred transmission map based on patient locations, time of infection, and genetic distances computed from sequencing data from bacterial isolates in 18 patients.
